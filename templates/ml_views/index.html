Predict Breast Cancer App

This app is to be utilized as a suplemental diagnostic tool for users to detect the type of cancer (malignant or benign) a patient is likely to have based on results from a digitized image of a Fine Needle Aspiration Biopsy (FNA). The images from this test describe characteristics of the cell nuclei present in the image. The main idea around this tool is to construct a diagnosis system that could achieve diagnostic accuracy comparable to that of a surgical biospy.
