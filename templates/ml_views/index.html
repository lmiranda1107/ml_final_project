Predict Breast Cancer App

This app provides an opportunity to detect the type of cancer (malignant or benign) a patient is likely to have based on results from a digitized image of a Fine Needle Aspiration Biopsy (FNA). They describe characteristics of the cell nuclei present in the image. The idea was to construct a diagnosis system that could achieve diagnostic accuracy comparable to that of a surgical biospy.
